Maps

The maps below represent the location references in 20 newspaper or periodical clippings from between 1814 and 1900. All clippings are available for download in the table below. Because of Google's restrictions on how many layers one can add to a map, clippings are separated by date. In the future, I hope to incorporate an API which will allow me to map more clippings simultaneously.

Click the button at the top left of each map to expand the layers. Places are limited to those directly mentioned in the article, and are often merely approximations given by the writer. While this sample of geographic data is too small to draw broad conclusions, individual stories are enhanced by the physical context of the map.

Some geodata was found using the fully GIS compliant 1869 map of London from Locating London's Past.
